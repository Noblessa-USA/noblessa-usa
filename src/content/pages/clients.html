Featured Clients

Explore our diverse portfolio of completed projects, showcasing our commitment to exceptional design and craftsmanship across a variety of styles.

The Youngers Residence

Los Angeles, CA

In the Hollywood Hills, the Youngers’ home is a true reflection of their artistic spirit, where noblessa’s refined craftsmanship meets a distinctly expressive design. The bespoke kitchen, custom bar, and curated entertainment center create a harmonious canvas that celebrates creativity, individuality, and timeless elegance.

The Lazars Home

Pasadena, CA

Step inside the Lazar Project, a California kitchen reimagined through a fusion of bold personal style and refined European design. Discover how noblessa helped bring a deeply personal vision to life in collaboration with interior designer Stephen Lazar.

The Silva Project

San Luis Obispo, CA

Humbled and inspired by the endless beauty of our local region, we strive to bring that same inspired spirit to each space. Seeing beyond the space to visualize the long-term use and functionality is key to bringing a concept to life. Our spaces pay tribute to the timeless integrity of nature through the seamless integration of hand-selected natural textures, shapes, and colors.

The Gran Cielo de Vina Robles

San Luis Obispo, CA

With the circle of seasons comes remarkable beauty in the vineyards of wine country. The Gran Cielo residence is a stunning collection of custom homes that exemplify how to bring the outdoors in, with expansive views and a design that celebrates the natural surroundings.

Haven Scottsdale

Scottsdale, AZ

Blending natural materials, refined finishes, and noblessa’s signature modern aesthetic, the Haven Scottsdale project creates a warm yet contemporary living environment. Each space is carefully tailored with a balance of rich wood tones, sleek matte surfaces, and high-quality stone elements to realize the unified vision of a modern desert residence.
