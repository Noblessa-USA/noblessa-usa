## Contemporary to Urban - Lighting for Every Style

The main lighting fixtures in your kitchen are crucial because they are the most stylized. Depending on your personal style, you may opt for simple recess lighting, a modern fixture that adds some flair, or bare bulbs with draping wires to give the space an industrial aesthetic.

In order to create a cohesive space, it's important to consider how your lighting feature will fit in with the rest of the decor within the room. If you prefer sleek white cabinetry, then perhaps simple, minimalistic lighting would suite your space well. On the other hand, if you're someone that likes to mix and match textures, patterns, and materials - such as wood and stone, then you might benefit from lighting that is bold and stands out.

There are many small areas within a kitchen that are forgotten about - such as the shelves, cabinets, and the island - when it comes to lighting. As a result, the space is not as functional and visible as it could be. In addition, creating light spaces means that you can turn all of these small nooks into a place to display all of your treasured possessions.

In each noblessa kitchen, you'll notice clever lighting solutions such as lights underneath shelving, glowing within frosted glass cabinets, and illuminating storage spaces within islands.
