## Benefit 3: Modular Kitchens

*noblessa’s modular kitchens are unique - specifically configured and positioned to fit your kitchen’s specific layout. Highly adaptable, and suitable for kitchens in every shape and size.* 

Modular Kitchens are an innovative solution that addresses issues ingrained in traditional designed kitchens. A modular kitchen is constructed in modules - think of it as smaller pre-built units that fit together like puzzle pieces. There are three different types of units that a modular kitchen is made from - wall units above your appliances, base units below your appliances, and tall units for pantries. The number of units grouped together and how they are configured can vary based on the size and layout of your kitchen. 

The most significant advantage when compared to traditional kitchens is that they can be assembled and reassembled. Meaning, if you ever want to relocate in the future, you can bring your modular kitchen with you to your new home! You can even add more units to scale your modular kitchen to a larger space or reconfigure based on your new home’s kitchen layout.
