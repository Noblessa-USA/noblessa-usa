While familiarity is nice sometimes, it can put a damper on nurturing curiosity. Since our brains [seek novelty](https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/blog/brain-reboot/202311/the-psychology-and-neuroscience-of-curiosity#:~:text=The%20neurotransmitter%20dopamine%20activates%20neurons,urge%20to%20explore%20new%20surroundings.) and our environment plays a huge role in mental health, home renovations are critical for mental stimulation and overall happiness.

Getting new kitchen and bathroom cabinets is a great way to spruce up your space. To make the most of these projects, you need to learn some cabinet design ideas, including:

- Functional designs
- Creative colors
- Luxury finishes

Read on to learn some must-have features in modern cabinetry solutions!

## 1. Sophisticated Sizes

Kitchen cabinets tend to be among the tallest and deepest in your home. Around 24 inches is the standard depth. This makes sense when you consider how many items they must hold, including:

- Pots and pans
- Kitchen utensils
- Dishware
- Glassware
- Cleaning supplies
- Serving pieces
- Dish towels
- Food supplies

Consider floor-to-ceiling cabinets for an elegant luxury look. Beautiful wood or glass cabinetry in all areas rounds out the room and ensures the design looks even.

Bathroom cabinets are generally shorter and deeper. They're usually 24-30 inches in height and 12-21 inches deep. If you want to save space in the bathroom, consider adding features above the cabinet, like integrated sinks or vanities.

![a noblessa modular kitchen](/assets/images/blog/november2024/7-must-have-features-in-modern-kitchen-and-bathroom-cabinets-2.webp)

## 2. Integrated Features

Whether you're looking into bathroom storage options or kitchen and bathroom cabinets, you can integrate other features into your cabinetry.

Sinks are a common fixture that you can incorporate into the structure of your custom cabinets. Consider a large glass bowl beneath a modern stainless steel fountain faucet.

In a kitchen, you may also install an engineered quartz or natural stone countertop on top of your cabinets. This gives you a space for meal prep and cooking conveniently located near your kitchenware.

Consider countertops with:

- Marble-look designs
- Sleek, bold edges
- Black bases and white veining
- Creamy warm colors (to go with softer lighting)
- Eggshell bases with bold veining (to go in contemporary kitchens)

Bathrooms don't have meal prep space, but you'll need a vanity to get ready in the morning. Integrate a vanity with a mirror above your bathroom cabinets. This saves you space and ensures that you always have a place to ensure you look your best.

## 3. Built-In Shelving

Some people have empty cabinets. They're just deep boxes that you stick things into. Unfortunately, this can get [extremely disorganized](https://www.simplyproductive.com/2022/05/7-psychological-benefits-of-an-organized-home/) when you're just throwing dishware or blow-dryers inside without any thought to where they go.

Shelving is among the most important custom cabinet features for a functional design. Put some shelves near the top of your kitchen cabinets to store spare cutlery or fancy dishware you use to entertain infrequently. Put rarely-used cosmetics here in bathrooms.

You can also add box-style cubicles to store different items. This is ideal for families that have several people who want their own storage space within the cabinets. It makes it easier for everyone to find their items without digging.

![a noblessa modular kitchen](/assets/images/blog/november2024/7-must-have-features-in-modern-kitchen-and-bathroom-cabinets-4.webp)

## 4. Pull-Out Drawers

Every high-quality cabinet has drawers above it. These drawers hold small objects that you use regularly. In kitchens, they usually hold:

- Daily cutlery
- Common cooking utensils
- Spices that you use often
- Day-to-day cleaning supplies

In bathrooms, you might include:

- Toothbrushes and toothpaste
- Hairbrushes
- Makeup
- Hair care items

Make sure that your drawers match the overall design of your cabinet. They should have a knob made from the same material as the cabinet's knob and hinges. This creates a cohesive aesthetic for a cleaner appearance.

## 5. Natural Wood

Wood cabinetry is a great choice for those who want to create a solid, modern look around their home. You can choose from warm-looking woods like:

- Oak
- Mahogany
- Cherry
- Hickory

There are also some sleek, modern woods with cool undertones. They include:

- Maple
- Pine
- Ash
- Poplar

Make sure that your wood cabinets are polished to perfection. You want them to look shiny and sleek.

You can take things a step further with intricate wood designs. Consider etchings in geometric shapes around the side of the cabinet for a seamless contemporary appearance. These borders are simple and go well with minimalistic decor.

![a noblessa modular kitchen](/assets/images/blog/november2024/7-must-have-features-in-modern-kitchen-and-bathroom-cabinets-6.webp)

## 6. Mood-Boosting Colors

Wood or PVC cabinets can be made in pretty much any color you like. For wooden varieties, this means choosing a unique paint hue.

One way to do this is to match your pre-existing kitchen decor. If you have light blue walls, consider painting your cabinets cornflower or midnight blue.

Monochromatic color schemes combine black and white with multiple shades or a single hue. These are the most elegant contemporary color schemes out there.

If your walls are a neutral hue like white or beige, you can use the cabinets to add a pop of color. The hues that you use in your kitchen greatly impact your overall mood and can alter your mental state. Consider:

- Calming blue for bathrooms where you want to relax
- Cheerful yellow for spaces where you want to inspire joy
- [Invigorating orange](https://www.colorpsychology.org/orange/) in kitchens where you actively cook and entertain

Experts can help you determine which colors best match your overall home feel.

![a noblessa modular kitchen](/assets/images/blog/november2024/7-must-have-features-in-modern-kitchen-and-bathroom-cabinets-7.webp)

## 7. Gorgeous Glass

If you want something unique and interesting, glass cabinetry is a great choice. Using clear glass lets you see inside the cabinets. You can create a visually engaging configuration of beautifully arranged kitchen or bathroom items.

However, you might not want to see inside every cabinet. Frosted glass works wonders here. It protects your privacy and affords a seamless look with consistent glass.

If you want something even more unique, consider:

- Checkerboard patterns made from clear and frosted glass
- Glass etchings in other geometric shapes
- Borders around the glass made from metal
