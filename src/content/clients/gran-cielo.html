Luxury Living, Elevated

Modern Elegance in Wine Country

Nestled within the rolling vineyards of Gran Cielo de Vina Robles, noblessa brings modern European elegance to a thriving community in the heart of California’s wine country. Designed for a lifestyle that blends tranquil vineyard living with refined sophistication, each space reflects a careful balance of natural oak textures, sleek matte finishes, and timeless Noblessa craftsmanship.

Located within 800 acres of stunning vineyards, Gran Cielo offers 42 custom home sites beside the Vina Robles Winery and Amphitheatre. With more than three hundred celebrated tasting rooms, artisan galleries, and open-air concerts nearby, this is where culture and comfort meet. Noblessa’s role in this project ensures each residence embodies European precision, timeless design, and California ease.
