Refined Everyday Living

The Kitchen as a Statement Piece

The main kitchen features noblessa’s walnut laminate cabinetry, paired with warm yellow gold handles and a two-level stacked configuration for optimal storage. The San Remo island serves as both centerpiece and workspace, enhanced by integrated Line N lighting. A paneled dishwasher beneath the window and hidden appliances preserve the seamless look. Black oak cabinetry defines the adjoining bar, contrasted with divine anti-fingerprint blue fronts accented by gold channels—an unexpected pairing that elevates the space.

Modern Floating Designs

Bathrooms with Character and Continuity

The master bathroom continues the walnut and gold motif, with floating cabinetry that integrates a locally sourced stone sink for a natural yet modern aesthetic. Secondary bathrooms use noblessa’s San Remo finish with black Line N channels, all in floating configurations that create a clean, contemporary look. Every detail—from push-to-open mechanisms to mirrored toe kicks—underscores noblessa’s blend of functionality and refined design.
